Facilities

Our school offers a wide range of exceptional facilities designed to support both academic excellence and personal growth. Our state-of-the-art classrooms are equipped with the latest technology to enhance learning, while our well-stocked libraries provide a quiet space for study and exploration. We also have dedicated science and computer labs, enabling hands-on experiences for students to engage with their subjects in practical, innovative ways. For sports enthusiasts, our modern sports facilities include an outdoor playing field, basketball courts, a gymnasium, and swimming pool, providing ample opportunities for physical development and teamwork. Additionally, the school has creative arts spaces, including art rooms, music studios, and drama halls, encouraging students to express themselves through various forms of art. Our school also prioritizes student well-being with comfortable, welcoming lounges, a health center staffed by professional nurses, and a nutritious cafeteria serving balanced meals. With these top-tier facilities, students are empowered to reach their full potential, both academically and personally.
