JUNIOR HIGH SCHOOL ADMISSION
This is to announce on the field of Admission and Screening for Senator Renato "Compañero" Cayetano Memorial Science and Technology High School for incoming Grade 7 students under Science, Technology, and Engineering (STE) Program AY. 2021-2022. The face-to-face entrance examination will not be administered in consideration of the quarantine restrictions that are currently in effect in the country. Instead, admission qualifications shall be derived mainly from the academic performance in Grade 5.

In adherence to the DepEd Order No. 55 s. 2010 on Policies and Guidelines on Strengthening Science and Mathematics Education in the Secondary level, we will be administering Admission and Screening to Incoming Grade 7 students for SY 2021- 2022 starting April 5, 2021 - April 30, 2021. Qualified applicants based on Grade Requirements will undergo scheduled interviews from May 3- May 28, 2021.

Must be a bona fide Grade 6 elementary student in any public or private school. Must have a final grade of: 85% or higher in three (3) major subjects – Science, English and Mathematics 83% for minor subjects, Must have no grades lower than 80% in any grading period. Must be of good moral character and have never been involved in any school violation.
