Courses We Offer

UBNHS provides a variety of academic programs designed to prepare students for the future.

Junior High School

Comprising Grades 7, 8, 9, and 10, our Junior High School program lays the foundation for lifelong learning and academic excellence.

Senior High School - STEM

The Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics strand equips students with knowledge and skills in science-related fields for future careers.

Senior High School - ABM

The Accountancy, Business, and Management strand is ideal for students who aspire to excel in the fields of business and entrepreneurship.

Senior High School - HUMSS

The Humanities and Social Sciences strand focuses on preparing students for courses in social work, education, and related fields.

Senior High School - GAS

The General Academic Strand offers flexibility for students exploring multiple career paths and academic disciplines.
