Senior High School admissions
This is to announce on the field of Admission and Screening for Senator Renato "Compañero" Cayetano Memorial Science and Technology High School for incoming Grade 7 students under Science, Technology, and Engineering (STE) Program AY. 2021-2022. The face-to-face entrance examination will not be administered in consideration of the quarantine restrictions that are currently in effect in the country. Instead, admission qualifications shall be derived mainly from the academic performance in Grade 5.

In adherence to the DepEd Order No. 55 s. 2010 on Policies and Guidelines on Strengthening Science and Mathematics Education in the Secondary level, we will be administering Admission and Screening to Incoming Grade 7 students for SY 2021- 2022 starting April 5, 2021 - April 30, 2021. Qualified applicants based on Grade Requirements will undergo scheduled interviews from May 3- May 28, 2021.

GUIDELINES FOR ADMISSION IN SCIENCE, TECHNOLOGY, AND ENGINEERING (STE) Program in Junior High School specifically for Incoming Grade 7 Students S.Y. 2021-2022z

Must be a bona fide Grade 10 junior high school student in any public or private school.

Must have a final grade of:

  • 85% or higher in three (3) major subjects – Science, English and Mathematics
  • 83% for minor subjects,
  • Must have no grades lower than 80% in any grading period.
  • Must be of good moral character and have never been involved in any school violation.

Submit the application requirements (the duly signed accomplished forms and other related documents in a long brown envelope) to the Registrars' Office or to the Guard-On-Duty.

On the upper left corner back side of the envelope, please properly label it with:

  1. The applicant's complete name (SURNAME, FIRST NAME, MIDDLE NAME)
  2. Contact number
  3. Personal or parents' email address
  4. Name of current school

Qualified applicants for enrolment shall be announced only after the scheduled interview to be conducted by a panel composed of Division Supervisors and Faculty members of the school.

The following criteria for admission screening is as follows:

  • 60% - Grade 9 Final Grade in Science, English and Mathematics
  • 30% - Interview
  • 10% - Certificate of Winnings in Science and Mathematics
  • Total of 100%
